--> In the top navigation bar you may return to the original state of the website which is the Disclaimer about the website by clicking on District Information | Disclaimer
--> You are reading the Help so you made it to the Help link in the navigation Bar.
--> Go to the OFFICIAL ECASD site by using the link in the navigation toolbar on the topof the site.
--> Go to the Wisconsin Department of Instruction website by using the WI DPI link in the top navigation bar.

--> Click on the Disclaimer Tab to review a bit about this map and the disclaimer related to it.

--> Click on the District Facts Tab to find information related to the District as a whole from the WI DPI Report Card.
--> Click on the High School Facts Tab to find information related to the District High Schools from the WI DPI Report Card.
--> Click on the Middle School Facts Tab to find information related to the District Middle Schools from the WI DPI Report Card.
--> Click on the Elementary School Facts Tab to find information related to the District Elementary Schools from the WI DPI Report Card.
Click on the dropdown on each of above 4 Tabs to change your variable.
There are tooltips with labels to assist you on the map.
--> Click on the Eau Claire City/County Facts Tab to find information related to the City or the County of Eau Claire.

--> Click on the School Locator Tab to do the following tasks -
Search for an address to identify what school you may attend. (Top right of map)
Turn each of the layers on and off in the Layers pane. (Second item in top right of map)
Identify the school location in the district by clicking on the icon. (Popup will appear when you click)
Identify the School boundary by clicking on the boundary. (Popup will appear when you click)

--> Click on the Sources Tab to find links to where the data for this site came from.
