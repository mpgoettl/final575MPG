This website and anything associated with it does not represent Eau Claire Area School District.

This website and interactive maps were created for the final project in Geography 575 at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, and completly independent of the ECASD. The creator nor the ECASD shall be held liable for ANY use of this website. The data used for this site was been taken from what is considered reliable sources but is presented "AS IS". The purpose of the whole project was to be able to Operate JavaScript, JQuery, d3 and the Leaflet.js mapping libraries. Assemble and dynamically load a spatiotemporal information set in the GeoJSON format and Implement operator primitives, including pan, zoom, retrieve and others.

The data for the District, High, Middle and Elementary Schools are based on the 2018-2019 school report cards, Information related to the 2019-2020 school report cards were not available due to the pandemic.

My hope is that this information can provide individuals with mkore information related to the ECASD and informationrelated to it.
